Haus of Decline is a podcast hosted by two guys called Jack Dump and snapdeus.

The show is a conversation on why it feels like the world around us is constantly devolving, like the infinite descent of a barber-pole’s spiral.

Each week, Jack and snapdeus talk about the news cycle or subjects of recent fascination and try to be funny, while also tying these subjects to their overarching theory of Historical Miserablism.

Recurring themes include: Calvinism, Weber’s definition of Statehood, The Commodification of Dissent, The Information Economy, Fascism as Nostalgia, Extremely Online Culture, GNU/Linux, and Kingdom Hearts.

Jack Dump makes the comics, and snapdeus makes the website.
